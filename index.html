Onboarding and Customer Master Update Processes

The onboarding and customer master update processes involve obtaining necessary approvals from legal compliance for Edison, ED, or the business unit, BU, controller for Westinghouse, WH, and submitting a request to the OpX team to load the information into Oracle.

The sequence of the onboarding and customer master update processes is:

1. First, the requestor obtains any necessary approvals from the Edison, ED, compliance group, or the Westinghouse, WH, business unit, BU, controllers.

2. Second, the requestor submits their onboard or customer master update request to the OpX team only after confirming that their approval submission has been authorized, or they can make the submission to OpX if the particular customer master update does not require approval.

3. Third, the OpX team loads the approved or general customer master details into Oracle.

The requestor can submit an onboarding or customer master update request via Salesforce Form, Microsoft Form, or email. A requestor should use email only if they do not have access to the Salesforce or Microsoft forms.

If a corresponding Salesforce case exists, the Salesforce form is the preferred method for submitting an onboarding request.

To send a request by email, use the FCS email address, freight.customerservice@wabtec.com, or the OPX email address, opx.helpdesk@wabtec.com.

When submitting a request through email, the requestor must provide complete and detailed information. Do not instruct the OpX team to reference attachments, files, cases, etc.

The Salesforce and Microsoft forms are preferred submission methods because they guide the requestor on what information the OpX team requires to perform the onboard or customer master update accurately, with little to no back and forth communication with the requestor to obtain clarifications.

 

 

Onboarding and Customer Master Update Roles and Responsibilities

The requestor is responsible for obtaining any necessary compliance approvals for Edison, ED, or controller approvals for Westinghouse, WH, before submitting a customer onboard or customer master update request to the OpX team.

The requestor can check the approval status of their Edison, ED, compliance request by accessing the account in Salesforce and reviewing the address section for the status as updated by the compliance group.

The OpX team cannot proceed with Oracle account or customer master changes that require approval without confirmation from the Edison, ED, compliance team, or the Westinghouse, WH, business unit, BU, controllers.

The OpX team's role in onboarding is limited to performing the Oracle setup, and OpX does not initiate approvals on the requestor s behalf.

Approval Requirements

In Westinghouse, WH, new ship-to addresses for existing or current customers do not require controller approval before entering.

In Westinghouse, WH, new customer accounts or new bill-to addresses for existing or current customers require controller approval before entering into Oracle. The exact requirement depends on the subject business unit, BU, controller.

In Edison, ED, all new ship-to and new bill-to addresses require compliance approval before entering.
